# Sales Performance Dashboard

![Sales Performance Dashboard](Screenshot%202026-04-19%20143252.png)

## 📊 Project Overview
This **Sales Performance Dashboard** was built in Microsoft Excel for the period **January – August 2026**.  
It provides a clear overview of sales metrics, revenue by category & city, payment methods, and monthly trends to support data-driven decisions.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track total revenue and number of orders
- Identify top-performing cities and product categories
- Analyze payment method distribution
- Monitor sales trends over time

  ## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains:
- Order ID
- Customer details (Name, Email, Phone, City)
- Product & Category
- Quantity & Unit Price
- Order Date
- Payment Method

## ✨ Key Features
- **Total Revenue**: ₦296,300
- **Total Orders**: 15
- **Average Order Value**: ₦19,753
- Interactive visualizations:
  - Revenue by Category (Bar)
  - Revenue by City (Horizontal Bar)
  - Revenue by Payment Method (Pie Chart)
  - Monthly Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
- Slicers for filtering by **City** and **Category**

## 🔍 Key Insights
- **Electronics** dominates with **₦161,500** — approximately **54.5%** of total revenue.
- **Lagos** is the highest revenue-generating city, followed by **Abuja**.
- **Card payments** lead at **43.13%**, followed by Cash (**35.44%**) and Transfer (**21.43%**).
- Revenue peaked strongly in **June and July**, then declined in **August**.
- High Average Order Value (**₦19,753**) despite only 15 total orders.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Prioritize marketing and stock for **Electronics** and key cities (**Lagos & Abuja**).
- Develop strategies (e.g., bundling or promotions) to boost **Accessories** and **Stationery**.
- Investigate the revenue drop in August to prevent future declines.
